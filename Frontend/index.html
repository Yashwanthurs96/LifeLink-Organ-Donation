Donation Facts

MYTH

"Doctors won't work as hard to save my life if they see I'm a donor."

FACT Medical teams prioritize saving your life first. Donation is only discussed after every life-saving measure is exhausted.

MYTH

"I am too old to be an organ donor."

FACT There is no cut-off age for donation. Organs have been successfully transplanted from donors in their 90s.
